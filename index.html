About Utubaa

Utubaa Electro Mechanical Engineering PLC Utubaa Electro Mechanical Engineering PLC stands today as one of Ethiopia's most trusted MEP contractors—a reputation forged not overnight, but through nearly three decades of resilience, reinvention, and relentless technical excellence. The company's roots trace back to 1995, when it was first established in the town of Kuyyu Gebre Gurracha under the original name Utuba M Mechanical & Electrical Works. With nothing more than a bold vision and an initial capital of just 200,000 Ethiopian Birr, the fledgling enterprise began its journey in a modest workshop, undertaking small-scale mechanical and electrical tasks with hands-on dedication. Over the years, through strategic reform, organizational maturity, and an unwavering commitment to quality, the company underwent a fundamental transformation. It was rebranded as Utubaa Electro Mechanical Engineering PLC—a name that reflects broader technical scope, professionalism, and a renewed corporate identity. Alongside this change, the company made a pivotal strategic decision: to relocate its central operations, including its main office and fully equipped workshops, to Suluta, Shegegar City—a growing industrial hub offering better infrastructure, client access, and room for expansion. Today, Utubaa Electro Mechanical Engineering PLC delivers end-to-end engineering solutions that span the full MEP spectrum and beyond. Its core competencies include: Industrial electrical systems – from power distribution to control panels HVAC systems – heating, ventilation, and air conditioning for commercial and industrial spaces Machine maintenance – preventive and corrective upkeep for production machinery Factory installation – complete electromechanical setup for manufacturing plants PLC programming and configuration – automation and process control tailored to industry needs Fire alarm systems – detection and safety solutions compliant with international standards Generator backup systems – reliable power redundancy for uninterrupted operations Across every project, Utubaa remains anchored to three uncompromising pillars: safety, efficiency, and reliability. Whether serving a small enterprise or a large industrial facility, the company brings engineering precision, local expertise, and a proven legacy of adapting and thriving—from a 200,000 Birr startup in Kuyyu Gebre Gurracha to a leading MEP contractor operating from Shegegar City, serving clients across Ethiopia..

Our mission is to provide sustainable electro-mechanical infrastructure that drives Ethiopia's construction and industrial growth. Fully licensed, insured, and certified.
